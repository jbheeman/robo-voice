The Westfield Innovation Center is a two-story community building designed for students, visitors, and local organizations. The main entrance faces Oak Street, and the reception desk is directly inside the lobby. Visitors must check in at the desk and receive a temporary badge before entering other areas.

Room 101 is on the first floor beside the main office. It is used for small meetings and tutoring sessions. The cafeteria is across from Room 101 and serves lunch from 11:30 a.m. to 1:30 p.m. The nearest restroom is next to the cafeteria.

The library is on the second floor near the east staircase. It is open from 8:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Students may borrow books for two weeks. The robotics lab is located in Room 204 across from the library. Safety goggles are required during experiments.

The auditorium is at the end of the second-floor hallway. Large presentations and school events are held there. The nurse’s office is on the first floor near the west entrance. In an emergency, visitors should contact reception or call building security using the phone beside the elevator. The elevator connects both floors and is available to all visitors during normal building operating hours daily.
